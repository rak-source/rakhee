Building Diverse, World-Class Technical Teams

In today’s fast-paced startup environment, companies across various industries—from pre-seed to Series C—are facing an intense demand for top-tier talent. These companies often need recruiting services that can help them scale quickly while maintaining a focus on building diverse, inclusive, and high-performing technical teams. Rakhee offers fractional recruiting services that provide the expertise, tools, and personalized approach required to meet these challenges head-on.

Creating Tailored Job Descriptions and Interview Processes

Rakhee helps companies craft job descriptions that attract the right candidates, using AI tools to optimize language, identify key trends, and ensure inclusivity. By leveraging AI, Rakhee ensures job postings appeal to a diverse talent pool while reflecting the company’s technical and cultural needs.

Additionally, Rakhee works with companies to develop structured interview processes that assess skills, diversity of thought, and cultural alignment, ensuring a fair and respectful experience for all candidates.

AI-Driven Sourcing: Enhancing Recruitment Precision

Rakhee leverages the latest AI tools to streamline sourcing, enabling faster and more precise identification of top talent. This approach not only saves time but also uncovers hidden talent.

AI tools help automate initial outreach, ensuring efficient candidate engagement while maintaining a personalized touch. By combining AI with human expertise, Rakhee provides a faster and more effective recruitment process, helping companies identify top-tier technical talent that aligns with their needs.

Full-Cycle Recruitment

Rakhee’s fractional recruiting services extend beyond just sourcing candidates. Rakhee manages the entire recruitment cycle, from finding top talent to closing the right candidates. By leveraging an extensive network and industry-specific knowledge, Rakhee identifies highly qualified individuals who are the best fit for the company’s technical and cultural needs.

The recruitment process is managed end-to-end, including initial outreach, screening, interviews, feedback gathering, and candidate selection. By overseeing all stages of recruitment, Rakhee ensures a smooth process for both candidates and hiring teams, which ultimately leads to faster hiring and better outcomes for companies.

Market Research and Salary Benchmarking

Compensation plays a significant role in attracting and retaining top talent. That’s why Rakhee conducts comprehensive market research and salary benchmarking for each role, ensuring that offers made to candidates are competitive within the industry. By providing data-driven insights into industry trends, compensation packages, and regional variances, Rakhee helps companies stay ahead of the curve and ensure that their compensation packages align with the market and their talent needs.

Offer Negotiations: Securing the Right Talent

Securing top talent often requires more than just offering the highest salary. Rakhee has negotiated over 400 offers throughout her career, maintaining an 85% close rate across all technical hiring. By maintaining transparent and effective communication throughout the negotiation process, Rakhee ensures that both parties are satisfied, ultimately resulting in a successful hire.

Rakhee also brings a strong understanding of equity and company valuation, allowing her to effectively communicate the long-term potential of equity compensation. She excels at selling the company’s upside, helping candidates understand the value of stock options and how they align with the company’s growth. This expertise ensures that candidates feel confident about their overall compensation package and the future potential of their role.

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) Integrations and Training

A seamless recruitment process requires a reliable and efficient system to manage candidates and streamline workflows. Rakhee specializes in setting up and integrating Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) for companies, including popular platforms like Greenhouse and Lever. Whether a company is new to ATS or needs an overhaul of its current system, Rakhee ensures that the system is implemented efficiently and effectively.

In addition to handling the technical aspects of ATS setup, Rakhee provides comprehensive training to in-house teams, ensuring that they are well-versed in using the software.
