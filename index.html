Rakhee Gupta

Technical Recruiting Leader and Partner at Monochrome

Rakhee is a seasoned talent acquisition leader with 12 years of experience in technical recruiting, specializing in fintech and bridging the gap between top-tier tech talent and industry-leading organizations. With a strong foundation built at Goldman Sachs, Google, and Bloomberg, Rakhee has brought her expertise to startups, helping to build technical teams from the ground up.

Earlier in her career, Rakhee joined Addepar as their first recruiter, scaling the NYC headquarters from 50 to 250 employees. She played a pivotal role in hiring across key engineering teams, including Software Engineering, Security, Platform, SRE, Product, Data Science, and Machine Learning.

Currently, Rakhee has partnered with Monochrome to recruit top technical talent for leading hedge funds in NYC and serves as a consultant to startups ranging from Pre-Seed to Series C, leveraging her deep industry expertise to drive growth and innovation.
